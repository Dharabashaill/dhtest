HEAD-TEACHER MESSAGE

Dharabashail High School was established in 1964.It is a traditional school located in Dharabashail village under Kotalipara upazila of Gopalganj district.
The founder of this school was late Mahendranath Biswas.More than three hundred students study in the school The main building of the school has four floors and the other building in the school campus is used as a flood shelter.
The school has a computer lab, a science lab and a well-stocked library.Along with education, sports and cultural programs are also celebrate here regularly.Every national day is observed according to proper rules.
